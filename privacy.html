Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

BumbleJobs HR Recruitment is committed to protecting the privacy of job seekers and candidates actively seeking jobs and internships. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full name, contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Resume/CV, educational background, and work experience
  • Skills, certifications, job preferences, and career goals
  • Any other information voluntarily provided when applying for jobs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with suitable job or internship opportunities
  • To communicate with you regarding job offers and interviews
  • To improve our recruitment and placement services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your details only for job placement purposes, with your prior consent. We may disclose your information if required by law.

5. Data Security

We use security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary. To update or delete your data, contact us at info@bumbblejobs.com.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing activities

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
